About Joy Alicia

Joy Alicia is a writer, stand-up comic, podcast host, and founder of Online Dating Savior. Her award-winning book So Drunk A Tooth's Gotta Go won the Writer’s Digest Humor Award and has been featured in Newsweek, Daily Mail, Metro UK and Blavity. She performs across California, Texas, and Oregon.

She once had 10,000 men swipe right on her Tinder profile—fuel for the funniest chapters in her book. She’s also known to cry-laugh through a breakup playlist at 3 a.m., then wake up and turn it into material the next night.

Interview topics: modern dating disasters, writing funny essays, turning embarrassment into material, podcasting about breakups.
