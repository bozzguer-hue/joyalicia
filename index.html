Meet Joy Alicia

Joy Alicia is a writer, stand-up comic, podcast host, and founder of Online Dating Savior. Her award-winning book So Drunk A Tooth's Gotta Go won the Writer's Digest Humor Award and has been featured in Newsweek, Daily Mail, Metro UK and Blavity.

Fun fact: She once had 10,000 men swipe right on her Tinder profile—fuel for the funniest chapters in her book. She's also known to cry-laugh through a breakup playlist at 3 a.m., then wake up and turn it into material the next night.

She performs across California, Texas, and Oregon, turning dating disasters into essays and stand-up—often while spoiling her avoidantly attached dog.

Interview topics: modern dating disasters, writing funny essays, turning embarrassment into material, podcasting about breakups, the gig economy, and finding humor in life's chaos.
