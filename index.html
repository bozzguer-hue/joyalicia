About Joy

Joy Alicia is a versatile podcast host, writer, stand-up comic, and founder of Online Dating Savior. Her writing has been published in Newsweek, The Daily Mail, Blavity, Metro UK, and The Bold Italic.

A passionate international traveler with a colorful dating history, Joy shares her unforgettable experiences with humorous flair. When Tinder notified her that 10,000 men had swiped right on her profile, it sparked the realization that her dating adventures could entertain others‚Äîand she hasn't looked back since.

She has performed stand-up comedy in California, Texas, and Oregon. Joy currently lives in southern California with her avoidantly attached dog.
